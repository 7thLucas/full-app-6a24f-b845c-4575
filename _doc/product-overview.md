# Product Overview

## What It Is

A web application for music school administrators to manage their school operations — scheduling lessons, tracking student progress, and coordinating recitals — with the strategic goal of keeping students progressing and parents engaged.

## Primary Users

**Music School Administrators** (studio owners, office managers, lead instructors)
- Run small-to-midsize music schools (typically 20–200 students)
- Juggle multiple instructors, varying lesson schedules, and parent communication
- Need visibility into student progress to prevent dropout and maintain satisfaction
- Organize periodic recitals and performance events

**Secondary stakeholders:**
- Instructors (who need their schedules and student rosters)
- Parents (who want visibility into their child's progress)

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Lesson Scheduling
- Calendar management for instructors and students
- Recurring lesson slots with exception handling
- Conflict detection and availability management
- Student assignment to instructors

### 2. Student Progress Tracking
- Per-student progress logs (skills mastered, pieces learned, milestones)
- Practice tracking and goal setting
- Instructor notes and feedback
- Progress reports visible to administrators and shareable with parents

### 3. Recital Management
- Event planning and scheduling
- Student performance assignments (who plays what, order)
- Parent invitations and attendance tracking
- Program notes and setlist management

## Strategic Goals

1. **Reduce student attrition** — progress visibility and consistent communication keep families engaged
2. **Save admin time** — centralized scheduling and automated reports replace spreadsheets and email threads
3. **Strengthen parent satisfaction** — transparent progress updates and organized recitals build trust

## Positioning

Not a billing/payments platform (focus is operational, not financial).
Not a learning management system with video lessons or curriculum (focus is administrative).

**This is:** An operations dashboard purpose-built for the unique workflow of music schools — where scheduling is complex (one-on-one lessons, varying instructor availability), progress is qualitative and instrument-specific, and recitals are high-stakes, high-touch events.

## Brand & Tone

- **Professional but warm** — approachable for small studio owners, credible for larger schools
- **Clear and calm** — reduce chaos, not add complexity
- **Progress-oriented** — the product exists to help students advance and stay engaged

## Scope & Boundaries

**In scope:**
- Lesson scheduling and calendar management
- Student progress tracking and instructor feedback
- Recital planning and coordination
- Basic parent visibility into student progress

**Out of scope (v1):**
- Payment processing and invoicing
- Online lesson delivery or video content
- Public-facing booking forms or self-service registration
- Advanced analytics or marketing automation

## Success Metrics

- Time saved per week on scheduling and coordination
- Student retention rate improvement
- Parent satisfaction with progress visibility
- Recital event coordination time reduction
