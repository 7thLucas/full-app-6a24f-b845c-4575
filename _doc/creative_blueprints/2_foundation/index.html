The Problem

Chaos behind the curtain

Music school administrators juggle complex one-on-one lesson schedules across multiple instructors, lose visibility into student progress between lessons, and coordinate high-stakes recitals using spreadsheets and email threads. The result: wasted time, student attrition, and frustrated parents.

The Solution

One dashboard, complete control

A purpose-built operations platform for music schools. Centralized lesson scheduling with conflict detection, transparent student progress tracking visible to parents, and streamlined recital coordination — all designed to reduce admin burden and keep students advancing.
