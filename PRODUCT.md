# Product Information: Music School Manager

## Brand & Positioning
**Professional but warm** — approachable for small studio owners, credible for larger schools. Clear and calm — reduce chaos, not add complexity. Progress-oriented — the product exists to help students advance and stay engaged.

## Primary Users
Music School Administrators (studio owners, office managers, lead instructors) who run small-to-midsize music schools (typically 20–200 students). They juggle multiple instructors, varying lesson schedules, and parent communication. Need visibility into student progress to prevent dropout and maintain satisfaction. Organize periodic recitals and performance events.

Secondary stakeholders: Instructors (who need their schedules and student rosters) and Parents (who want visibility into their child's progress).

## Core Strategic Principles
1. Reduce student attrition — progress visibility and consistent communication keep families engaged
2. Save admin time — centralized scheduling and automated reports replace spreadsheets and email threads
3. Strengthen parent satisfaction — transparent progress updates and organized recitals build trust

## Key Features
1. **Lesson Scheduling:** Calendar management, recurring lesson slots, conflict detection, student-instructor assignment
2. **Student Progress Tracking:** Per-student progress logs, practice tracking, instructor notes, shareable progress reports
3. **Recital Management:** Event planning, student performance assignments, parent invitations, program notes

## Anti-References
Not a billing/payments platform. Not a learning management system with video lessons. This is an operations dashboard purpose-built for music schools.
